Hunt Awards

Awards are given usually at the end of the formal hunting season, at the Annual Meeting or at Closing Hunt, except for those perpetual trophies that are part of riding competitions.

Masters' Fox Mask Award

Awarded once a season by the Santa Fe Hunt Joint Masters of Foxhounds, this trophy acknowledges members who have demonstrated extraordinary service and dedication to the hunt. This trophy was created in honor of Mrs. Fran Joswick, retired SFH member and founder of the Therapeutic Riding Program in San Juan Capistrano.
