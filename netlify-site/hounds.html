Forever Homes

Retirement & Adoption

Every so often, a hound is ready to retire, or discovers that the hunting life just isn't for them. When that happens, we look for the right home to take them in and give them the retirement — or the second career — they deserve.

We don't have any hounds available for adoption at the moment, but we're always glad to hear from people interested in giving a retired hound a home. Reach out through our contact form and we'll keep you in mind the next time a hound is looking for a family.
