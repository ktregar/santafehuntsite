Clinic · Sat, July 25 · Mountain Center, CA
Rattlesnake Avoidance Clinic

Presented by Natural Solutions, this clinic trains dogs to recognize and avoid rattlesnakes using safely muzzled live snakes paired with a remote training collar to build a lasting avoidance response. It's open to any breed or size of dog six months or older — a great fit for hunt hounds, farm dogs, and trail companions alike. Natural Solutions recommends annual refreshers for the first three to four years for the strongest long-term protection. Visit socalrattlesnakeavoidancetraining.com to learn more.

The exact address in Mountain Center will be sent to registered participants. Payment is accepted by cash, check, Venmo, or Zelle — no deposit is required to hold your spot. Please RSVP by July 20.

The Hunt Season

The formal hunt season runs November through April, with hounds going out two or three times a week in Riverside County. Hunt days are listed in the Members area calendar. Guests may cap (ride as a guest) with advance notice — contact us to arrange.
