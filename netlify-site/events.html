Santa Fe Hunt Hunter Pace

Date
May 30, 2026
Saturday
Time
8:30 AM – 2:00 PM
Check-in begins at 8:30
Location
Costo Ranch
Anza, CA 92539

An East-Coast-style competition open to all levels of rider. Teams of 2 or 3 navigate open terrain and optional jumps, aiming to complete the course as close as possible to an ideal time — no one knows what the ideal time is until the end. It's a test of horsemanship, not speed.

Four divisions accommodate English and Western riders of varying experience. Vendors will be on site, followed by a catered lunch and ribbon awards ceremony.

What to Know
  • ASTM-certified helmets required for all participants
  • Non-members must complete the digital liability waiver before riding
  • Teams of 2 or 3 riders
  • English and Western welcome — four divisions available
  • Lunch and vendors included

The Hunt Season

The formal hunt season runs November through April, with hounds going out two or three times a week in Riverside County. Hunt days are listed in the Members area calendar. Guests may cap (ride as a guest) with advance notice — contact us to arrange.
