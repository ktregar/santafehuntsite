Hunt News · June 2026

Welcome, Dr. Steven Thomas

A message from our new Honorary Huntsman to the Santa Fe Hunt membership.

Thank you to Masters Paine and Manser for their confidence in appointing me as Honorary Huntsman. I’m excited to work with the SFH hounds and members!

My wife Alisa and I are thrilled to be in southern California, nearer to our children and away from the challenging weather of the Midwest. We have two sons in California (and two grandsons) and a daughter in Maine so we will be splitting our time between the coasts.

As for me, I’ve been riding horses for much of my life but only started riding English and fox hunting about a quarter century ago. I’ve served as a whipper-in at three hunts and a Huntsman for nine seasons at the Fort Leavenworth Hunt, where I was also a Joint Master. I’ve also served as Guest Huntsman at the Belle Meade Performance Trial twice, Trial President at several trials, and have judged many more. I’ve served as Chairman of the MFHA Hound Show and Performance Trial Committee for the last five and a half years and was recently elected President of the Virginia Foxhound Club, where I will be heavily involved in the Virginia Hound Show in the future. I’ve also judged most of the hound shows in the US, as well as the North Island Hound Show in New Zealand in 2025.

I look forward to meeting the SFH members and would like to encourage your involvement with the training and exercising of the pack over the summer. Our staff team is hoping more members will share our passion for getting “up close and personal” with the hounds! We look forward to a great year of sport and fun and hope what we provide will entice more riders and hound lovers to join and become involved as the season unfolds.

I have created a new e-mail address, and I encourage you to reach out whenever you have questions or offers to support the hounds. That address is santafehuntsman@gmail.com.

Warm regards,

Steve
